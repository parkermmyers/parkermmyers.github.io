About

I've worked as a journalist since I was writing crappy "news" stories for the middle school yearbook staff, and since then I've bounced all around within the field.

Journalism at its essence is storytelling, which I've done in a variety of different mediums - the list includes newspaper writing, opinion writing, television reporting, television anchoring, radio reporting, and radio anchoring.

I've got experience with film and digital photography, video shooting and editing, copywriting, and basic advertising/marketing that utilized social media campaigns.

But all of this eventually led me to become a graphic designer, and I've never felt so much passion for what I do. I obsess over typography (especially free fonts) and read blogs about rebranding campaigns. I'm currently branding my own wedding, if that's any indication of how much I love this.

Outside of my career, I play guitar in the church worship band and I'm engaged to a beautiful, beautiful woman. I worship Google products.
