About

Happily married and playing guitar every Sunday morning.

I'm very lucky to call this a career. I obsess over typography (especially free fonts) and read blogs about branding campaigns - I branded my own wedding, if that's any indication of how much I love this. I love nailing the little details - nuances in typography, subtle hover effects, tiny differences in a grayscale color palette that introduce hierarchy to a design.

I'm almost always available for any freelance work - large or small. Feel free to email with any questions!

In short, I'm a designer that specializes in branding, web, print, and illustration - in that order.
