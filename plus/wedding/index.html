They Met

Emily and Parker met at a young age at church - Emily's mother, Annette, was Parker's preschool teacher. In 2008, Parker started to fall for the girl in his small group he’d known his whole life, but little did he know, Emily was smitten as well. They went on a few awkward dates a few weeks later and became official on January 10, 2009.

They Fell in Love

The two spent over six years together dating. They created music together. They traveled to Guatemala on a mission trip and spoke horrible spanish to local people. They took long road trips in Parker’s truck listening to John Mayer.

College forced a long-distance relationship on them. Parker graduated from IU as a graphic designer; Emily from Ball State as a child life specialist. They grew stronger as a team over time.

They Got Engaged

After finding Emily’s highly curated and frequently updated wedding Pinterest board, Parker took a hint, went out and bought a diamond ring. On August 11, 2014, Emily and Parker got engaged in their hometown in a pavilion glowing with candles and lights. He serenaded her with an acoustic song, got down on one knee, and proposed... Of course, she said “yes.”
